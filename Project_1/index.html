For my designs, I was very interested in translational and rotational motion representing time. I chose to execute a plane flying over the US from California to NYC every 6 hours. In addition, I chose basic shapes translating a given distance every minute or hour. In doing so, I demonstrated my perception of time as linear but resetting depending unit of concern. Finally, I chose a very simple clock that displays the current year, month, day, hour, minute, and second. I followed the analog that year is a larger unit and should consequently have the largest font, while being the first thing seen.
